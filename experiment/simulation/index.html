Calculation Formulas

Reynolds Number (Re)

Re = ρVD/μ
where:
ρ = fluid density (kg/m³)
V = flow velocity (m/s)
D = pipe diameter (m)
μ = fluid viscosity (Pa·s)

Darcy Friction Factor (f)

f = 64/Re (laminar flow)
f = 0.316/Re^0.25 (turbulent flow)

Head Loss (hf)

hf = f(L/D)(V²/2g)
where:
L = pipe length (m)
g = gravitational acceleration (9.81 m/s²)
